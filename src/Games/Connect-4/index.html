Instructions

  • Two players take turns, with one using red discs and the other using yellow discs.

  • The goal is to be the first player to connect four of their own discs in a row, either horizontally, vertically, or diagonally on the game grid.

  • Players take turns dropping one of their discs into any column of the grid.

  • After each move, players need to strategically place their discs to build a line of four of their own color. The line can be vertical, horizontal, or diagonal. If a player achieves this, they win the game.

  • If the entire grid is filled without any player achieving a four-in-a-row connection, the game is considered a draw. In this case, you can restart the game and play again.
